ASSISTANT:
I have reviewed the uploaded contract ("FL Records Management Association 5.15.2025 Agreement updated 10.30.23") and cross-referenced it with sample clauses from the uploaded library. Below are the suggested improvements and recommendations for critical areas:

---

### **1. Guest Room Block and Rates**
- **Improvement Suggestion**: The contract currently does not explicitly address a mechanism for adjusting room blocks in case of unforeseen lower attendance. Include a clause for **flexible room block adjustments** with a defined minimum commitment to avoid attrition penalties.
  - **Example Clause**: *The Group may reduce the number of contracted rooms by up to 20% without penalty if notified by [insert date]. Attrition charges will apply only to reductions exceeding this threshold.*

---

### **2. Cancellation Policy**
- The cancellation terms provided are clear but do not address **force majeure** or unforeseen circumstances (e.g., natural disasters, pandemics).
  - **Example Clause Addition**: From the library:
    - *"In the event that either party cannot fulfill the terms of this Agreement due to acts of God, government restrictions, or other unforeseen circumstances beyond reasonable control, both parties will be released from their obligations without penalties."*

---

### **3. HelmsBriscoe Placement Fee**
- Ensure transparency around the placement fee and its implications for both parties.
  - **Recommendation**: Explicitly mention that the placement fee does not influence the negotiated rates for rooms, nor does it apply to rooms booked outside the contracted block.

---

### **4. Audit of Attendees (G.R.I.P.)**
- While this section is comprehensive, consider strengthening language around **data privacy and attendee consent**, particularly in compliance with privacy laws.
  - **Improved Language**: *"Group is responsible for obtaining necessary consents from attendees for data sharing as per applicable data protection regulations. Hotel will ensure the secure handling of any shared information and will not disclose data beyond the purposes outlined in this Agreement."*

---

### **5. Parking Rates and Amenity Delivery**
- Include a clause to ensure that rates for parking and other amenities are locked for the event dates to prevent unexpected cost increases.
  - **Example Clause**: *"Parking and amenity delivery fees listed in this Agreement are guaranteed and will not increase during the event period."*

---

### **6. Concessions and Complimentary Benefits**
- Include a clause clarifying **concession adjustments** in case of revenue shortfalls.
  - **Example Clause**: *"In the event the Group's revenue does not meet 80% of the anticipated minimum, the Hotel reserves the right to adjust the concessions proportionately. Any adjustments will be communicated in advance and require mutual agreement."*

---

### **7. ADA Compliance**
- The contract should explicitly reference compliance with ADA requirements for public spaces and services.
  - **Example Clause**: *"Hotel agrees to comply with the Americans with Disabilities Act and provide reasonable accommodations for attendees with disabilities upon prior notification."*

---

### **8. Hidden Fees**
- Specify that any additional fees (e.g., resort fees, service charges) must be disclosed and agreed upon in advance.
  - **Example Clause**: *"The Hotel will not impose additional fees beyond those outlined in this Agreement unless agreed upon in writing by both parties."*

---

### **9. Check-In/Check-Out Policies**
- Late check-out charges and policies for early arrivals are clearly defined. However, consider including **guaranteed room availability** for VIP attendees or early arrivals to enhance service levels.

---

### **10. Risk Assessment**
- Quantify risks based on the contract clauses. Here’s a summary:
  - **High Risk**: Cancellation policy (missing force majeure).
  - **Medium Risk**: Attrition clauses (room block adjustment flexibility).
  - **Low Risk**: Parking and amenity rates (subject to unexpected changes).

---

### **11. Summary Recommendations**
- Add missing clauses for **force majeure** and flexible attrition.
- Strengthen data privacy provisions in the G.R.I.P. section.
- Include lock-in provisions for parking and amenity rates.
- Clarify language around HelmsBriscoe's placement fee and its implications.

### **ADDENDUM TO CONTRACT**  
**Between Hilton Daytona Beach Oceanfront Resort (“Hotel”) and Florida Records Management Association (“Group”)**  
**Date:** [Insert Date]  
**Reference Contract ID:** FL Records Management Association 5.15.2025 Agreement updated 10.30.23

---

#### **1. Flexible Room Block Adjustment**  
In the event that Group’s attendance forecast changes, the Group may reduce the number of contracted rooms by up to 20% without penalty if notified by Hotel no later than [insert date]. Attrition charges will only apply to reductions exceeding this threshold.

---

#### **2. Force Majeure Clause**  
If the performance of this Agreement is rendered impossible, illegal, or otherwise impracticable due to acts of God, government regulation, disaster, pandemic, or other unforeseen circumstances beyond the control of either party, the affected party shall be excused from performance without penalty. In such an event, the parties will use their best efforts to reschedule the event or renegotiate the terms of this Agreement.

---

#### **3. Parking and Amenity Rates Lock-In**  
The parking rates and amenity delivery fees outlined in this Agreement are guaranteed and will not increase during the contracted event dates of May 19-23, 2025. Any additional fees for services not outlined must be disclosed and agreed upon in writing prior to the event.

---

#### **4. Data Privacy and G.R.I.P. Compliance**  
The Group agrees to obtain the necessary permissions from attendees to share data with the Hotel for the purposes of the G.R.I.P. audit. The Hotel agrees to handle all shared data in compliance with applicable privacy laws and solely for purposes specified in this Agreement. No data will be disclosed to third parties without prior written consent from the Group.

---

#### **5. Adjustments to Concessions**  
If the actual total sleeping room revenue and food and beverage revenue for the Group’s event materializes at less than 80% of the anticipated minimum, the Hotel reserves the right to proportionately adjust concessions. Adjustments will be communicated in advance and require mutual agreement. The Group may elect to retain all concessions by paying for the difference between achieved and anticipated revenue.

---

#### **6. Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA) Compliance**  
The Hotel agrees to comply with the Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA) to ensure accessibility for all attendees. Group shall notify the Hotel of any specific accessibility requirements in advance, and the Hotel shall use best efforts to accommodate these needs.

---

#### **7. Transparency on Fees**  
The Hotel confirms that no additional charges (e.g., resort fees, service fees) beyond those outlined in this Agreement will be imposed without prior written consent from the Group.

---

#### **8. Guaranteed Availability for VIP Attendees**  
The Hotel will guarantee room availability for up to [insert number] VIP attendees for early arrivals or late check-outs, subject to prior notification from the Group no later than [insert date].

---

### **Approval and Execution**
This Addendum is incorporated into the original Agreement by mutual consent and agreement of both parties. It supersedes any conflicting clauses in the original Agreement and will be binding upon signature by both parties.
