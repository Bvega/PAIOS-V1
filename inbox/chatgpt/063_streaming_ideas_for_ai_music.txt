## 🪩 2. Streaming Platform Strategy

### **A. YouTube**
- Upload each version as a separate “Episode” in a playlist called *The [Song Title] Experiments*.
- Use end-screen polls or community posts for votes.
- Post short “genre reveal” teasers as YouTube Shorts.

### **B. Spotify / Apple Music / Deezer**
- Group all versions under one *EP* (same cover, different sub-titles).
  - Example: “Allí Queda Todo (Tango Version)”, “Allí Queda Todo (Afro-Bachata Version)”.
- Add a survey link in your artist bio (using Linktree or Koji) for audience voting.
- Once the winner is picked, release a *“Fan Edition”* remix as a follow-up single.

### **C. TikTok & Instagram Reels**
- Drop short clips of each version with the same lyric hook.  
- Encourage users to comment “1, 2, or 3” to vote for their favorite.
- Remix challenge: “Show me your vibe to Version 2” → promotes virality.

Add cinematic consistency: same lyrics, different musical universe. This gives you a **signature brand identity** — *the polymorphic artist.*
