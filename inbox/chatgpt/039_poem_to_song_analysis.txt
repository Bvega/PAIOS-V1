---

### 📻 **What is a Radio Edit?**
A **radio edit** is a **shorter, tighter version** of a song designed to fit on radio playlists and grab attention fast. It usually:

- ⏱ Runs **under 3 minutes** (often 2:30–2:50)
- 🔁 Includes **one verse + chorus + bridge or hook** — no long intros or outros
- 🚫 Removes anything **explicit, slow-building, or experimental**
- 🎯 Focuses on **the hook** — the part that gets stuck in your head

**Why do it?**  
To make the song more accessible and replayable — great for platforms like TikTok, Instagram Reels, or getting on curated playlists.

---

### 💃 **What is a Club Remix?**
A **club remix** takes the **emotional or lyrical core** of a song and reimagines it for **dance floors** or **live DJ sets**. It usually:

- ⏱ Is **longer** (3 to 6 minutes)
- 🥁 Has a **stronger beat**, more **bass**, and danceable rhythm
- 🎧 Uses **repetition**, **build-ups**, and **drops**
- 🔄 May loop phrases, chop up vocals, or shift the original vibe

**In your case:**  
Your tango-bachata-jazz fusion could become a **"downtempo Latin club remix"** — keeping the sensual feel but adding rhythm-forward energy. Think: bass-driven tango guitar, extended breakdowns, looping vocal phrases.
