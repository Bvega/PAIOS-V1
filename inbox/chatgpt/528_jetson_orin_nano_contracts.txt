Explain in simple word what is quantization and quantized models

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

ASSISTANT:
юИГQuantization is a technique used in machine learning to make models more efficient by reducing the precision of the numbers they use.юИД юИГTypically, models operate with 32-bit floating-point numbers (`float32`), which are precise but require significant memory and computational power.юИД юИГQuantization simplifies these numbers to lower-precision formats, such as 8-bit integers (`int8`), thereby decreasing the model's size and speeding up its operations.юИД юИАciteюИВturn0search0юИБюИЖ

юИГA **quantized model** is one that has undergone this process.юИД юИГBy converting its weights and activations to lower-precision numbers, the model becomes more lightweight and faster, making it suitable for deployment on devices with limited resources, like smartphones or embedded systems.юИД юИГWhile this reduction in precision can lead to a slight decrease in accuracy, the trade-off often results in a more practical and efficient model for real-world applications.юИД юИАciteюИВturn0search3юИБюИЖ

юИГIn essence, quantization helps in compressing machine learning models, enabling them to run efficiently on a wider range of hardware without significantly compromising performance.юИДюИЖ
