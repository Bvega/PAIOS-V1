# Philosophy for an Employee-Owned Tech Company  
*(70% employee-owned, 30% company reserve)*

### 1. **Shared Ownership, Shared Responsibility**
- Employees must understand they are not just workers but co-owners.  
- Ownership creates accountability: every decision, from coding standards to customer service, impacts their dividend.  
- Foster a *stewardship culture* rather than a *job mentality.*

---

### 2. **Clear Value Split: 30% Company / 70% Employees**
- **30% Company Retention**  
  - Reinves​t into R&D, scaling, infrastructure, legal protection, and market expansion.  
  - Acts as a long-term growth fund to keep the company sustainable.  
- **70% Employee Distribution**  
  - Distributed **proportionally to investment + contribution.**  
  - Formula:  
    - **Base Dividend** (equal share per employee-owner).  
    - **Merit Dividend** (performance and contribution weight).  
    - **Equity Dividend** (based on original capital invested).

This balance avoids rewarding only tenure or only capital, keeping innovation alive.

---

### 3. **Long-Term Wealth Creation**
- Create an **Employee Equity Trust**: shares are held in a trust, preventing hostile takeovers.  
- Employees earn dividends yearly but can also build long-term equity that matures with company growth.  
- Protect against “short-term cash grabs” by requiring part of dividends (e.g., 20%) to be reinvested automatically.

---

### 4. **Democratic but Structured Governance**
- Each employee-owner gets **one vote** for major company directions (not proportional to investment).  
- Leadership (CEO, board) is elected from within, but must have **professional qualifications**.  
- Avoid “committee paralysis”: use representative councils (5–7 members) rather than full group decision on daily operations.

---

### 5. **Transparency as a Core Value**
- Quarterly open financial reports shared with all employee-owners.  
- Salary bands, performance metrics, and dividend formula are **clear and open.**  
- This builds trust and prevents internal rivalries.

---

### 6. **Profit With Purpose**
- Define a **mission beyond money** (innovation, sustainability, social impact).  
- Employees align with a vision, not just profits—this keeps morale and retention high.  
- Example: set aside **1–2% of annual profits** for community or educational initiatives decided by employees.

---

### 7. **Resilience Over Greed**
- Market cycles hit tech companies hard.  
- The 30% retained earnings should serve as a **stability buffer** during downturns.  
- This ensures employee dividends don’t collapse in a bad year and protects jobs.

---

✅ **Guiding Philosophy Statement**:  
*"We are stewards, not just employees. Our work builds value that we share together, while securing the company’s future through reinvestment, transparency, and collective governance."*
