### Key Components:
1. **Personal Branded Landing Page**:
   - When someone scans your QR code (via a mobile device or a physical smart card), they are directed to a personal branded landing page on their device's browser.
   - This page contains your headshot, contact information, web bio link, LinkedIn profile, and more.
   - Additionally, it includes company promotional content like videos and links to websites or social media pages.

2. **Downloadable Contact Information**:
   - The contact can easily save all your details by clicking a "Download Contact" button, which directly adds the information to their mobile device.

3. **Multiple Card Options**:
   - **Digital-Only Option**: A digital card that can be added to iPhone/Android’s Apple/Google Wallet, functioning as a QR code that is scanned to open the branded landing page.
   - **Physical Smart Card**: Another option allows a physical card with the same QR code functionality.

4. **User License Fee**:
   - There is a yearly fee for using this service, and the digital card requires only the license cost with no additional physical card costs.

5. **Company Branding**:
   - The branded landing page also contains a company video and links to social media/website, helping promote the company alongside personal branding.

6. **Easy Setup**:
   - Users can create an account (e.g., via Mobilo), provide payment information, and customize their digital card.

### Steps to Emulate:
1. **Create the QR Code-Linked Personal Landing Page**:
   - Develop a system where each card, whether physical or digital, leads to a personalized landing page for the user.
   - Include essential details like headshot, contact info, social links, and a way to download contact info.
   - Embed company branding elements like videos and links for enhanced marketing.

2. **Digital and Physical Card Integration**:
   - Offer both digital cards that can be added to mobile wallets and physical smart cards. 
   - Ensure QR codes work seamlessly across all mobile devices.

3. **Licensing Structure**:
   - Implement a user licensing fee model, potentially with different tiers depending on card options (digital-only or physical).

4. **Customization Portal**:
   - Create a portal for users to easily set up, manage, and customize their digital cards, handling everything from contact details to payment info.

5. **Seamless Sharing and Contact Saving**:
   - Focus on the user experience of scanning, accessing the landing page, and saving contact information efficiently.

This setup can be extended to include additional features like analytics (e.g., tracking how many people scan the QR code) or more detailed personalization options for both users and companies.
