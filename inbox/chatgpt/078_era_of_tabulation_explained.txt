✅ **The Era of Tabulation**

### Explanation:
The **Era of Tabulation** refers to the period when machines first started helping humans sort and organize large amounts of data to find patterns and insights — going beyond simple counting or arithmetic.  

This era began in the late 19th and early 20th centuries, marked by inventions like **Herman Hollerith’s tabulating machine**, which was used for the 1890 U.S. Census. These electromechanical systems used punched cards to **categorize and analyze** data, laying the foundation for modern data processing and analytics.
