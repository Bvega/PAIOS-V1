**[Doctor’s Letterhead]**  
[Doctor’s Name]  
[Doctor’s Practice Name]  
[Address]  
[City, State, ZIP Code]  
[Phone Number]  
[Email Address]  

**[Date]**  

**To Whom It May Concern,**  

**Re: Certification of Capability for Living Aid Care**  

I, Dr. [Doctor’s Full Name], am a licensed [Specialty, e.g., General Practitioner, Family Physician] currently practicing at [Practice Name or Address]. I am writing this letter to certify that [Caregiver’s Full Name], residing at [Caregiver’s Address], is capable of providing living aid care for [Patient’s Full Name].  

Based on my professional assessment, [Caregiver’s Full Name] possesses the necessary physical, mental, and emotional attributes required to meet the caregiving needs of [Patient’s Full Name]. [Caregiver’s Full Name] has demonstrated the following:  
- Understanding of [Patient’s Full Name]'s condition and care requirements.  
- The ability to administer proper daily assistance and adhere to any medical instructions provided.  
- A commitment to ensuring the patient’s well-being, comfort, and safety.  

This certification is issued with confidence that [Caregiver’s Full Name] will provide quality care and maintain the standards necessary to support [Patient’s Full Name] in their living aid needs.  

Should further information be required, please do not hesitate to contact my office at [Phone Number] or via email at [Email Address].  

Sincerely,  

[Doctor’s Full Name]  
[Doctor’s Title and Credentials]  
[Doctor’s Signature]  

--- 

This letter can be adjusted as needed based on the specifics of the caregiver, patient, and care requirements.
