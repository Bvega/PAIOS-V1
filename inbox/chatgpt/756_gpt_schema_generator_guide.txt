define what is an schema in a very simple way

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

ASSISTANT:
A **schema** is a blueprint or plan that defines the structure of something. For example:  

- In a **database**, it tells how data is organized, like tables and their relationships.  
- In a **JSON file**, it shows the format of the data, like what fields exist and what kind of values they have.  

Think of it as a map that helps organize and understand complex information.
