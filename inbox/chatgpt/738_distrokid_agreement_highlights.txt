ASSISTANT:
A **digital fingerprint** is like a unique "signature" for a piece of music, created by analyzing its audio. It's not the actual sound or a recording but a **mathematical summary** of the song's key features, such as melody, rhythm, or beats. 

This fingerprint can be used to:
- Identify songs quickly, even if parts of them are modified or used in a video.
- Prevent unauthorized copies or uploads on platforms like YouTube.

Think of it as a "musical barcode" that helps track and protect your work.
