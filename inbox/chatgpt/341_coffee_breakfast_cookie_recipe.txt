### ☕ **Coffee Companion Breakfast Cookies**

#### 🍪 Ingredients (makes ~18 cookies):
- **1 cup** rolled oats  
- **1/2 cup** whole wheat flour (or all-purpose flour)  
- **1/2 tsp** baking powder  
- **1/2 tsp** baking soda  
- **1/4 tsp** salt  
- **1 tsp** cinnamon  
- **1/3 cup** brown sugar (or coconut sugar for less refined option)  
- **1/2 cup** peanut butter (or almond butter)  
- **1 large egg**  
- **1/4 cup** strong brewed coffee, cooled  
- **1/4 cup** melted butter or coconut oil  
- **1 tsp** vanilla extract  
- **1/3 cup** chopped nuts (e.g., walnuts or pecans – optional)  
- **1/3 cup** mini dark chocolate chips or dried cranberries (optional)

---

#### 👩‍🍳 Instructions:

1. **Preheat oven** to 350°F (175°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
2. In a large bowl, **mix dry ingredients**: oats, flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt, and cinnamon.
3. In another bowl, **whisk wet ingredients**: brown sugar, peanut butter, egg, coffee, melted butter/oil, and vanilla until smooth.
4. **Combine wet and dry** mixtures. Stir until well mixed. Fold in nuts or chips if using.
5. **Scoop** ~1.5 tbsp of dough and place onto baking sheet. Slightly flatten with your fingers or a spoon.
6. **Bake** for 10–12 minutes until edges are golden and centers are set.
7. Let cool for 5 minutes on tray, then transfer to a wire rack.

---

### 🍽️ Serving Tips:
- Great with hot coffee or a café con leche.
- Store in an airtight container up to 5 days, or freeze for grab-and-go mornings.
