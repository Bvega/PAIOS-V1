To create a MidJourney prompt that generates a realistic photograph of an ethereal 1920’s lady in black and white film with daylight, we need to incorporate specific elements that highlight the ethereal and vintage qualities, as well as ensure the use of black and white photography. Here's how you can structure the prompt:

---

**MidJourney Prompt:**

```
/imagine An ethereal woman from the 1920s, dressed in elegant vintage fashion, wearing a cloche hat and a flowing dress, captured in a black and white film style. The photograph should convey a sense of timeless elegance and grace, with soft daylight illuminating her features, casting delicate shadows. Her expression is serene and contemplative, with a slight smile. The setting is outdoors, with hints of an Art Deco architectural background, enhancing the 1920s ambiance. Use a cinematic still-shot with a close-up eye-level perspective to emphasize the details and textures of her attire and the subtleties of her expression. The overall atmosphere should be dreamy and nostalgic. --ar 3:2 --v 5.2 --style 4b --q 2 --no color
```

### Explanation of Parameters:
- **`--ar 3:2`**: This sets the aspect ratio to 3:2, which is common for portrait photography and allows for a well-balanced composition.
- **`--v 5.2`**: Indicates the version of MidJourney, which influences how prompts are interpreted.
- **`--style 4b`**: Specifies the style; `4b` is often used for black and white photography within MidJourney.
- **`--q 2`**: Sets the quality of the image generation, with higher values increasing the processing time but producing more detailed results.
- **`--no color`**: Ensures that the image remains in black and white, enhancing the vintage film effect.

### Additional Tips:
- **Ethereal Atmosphere**: Use terms like "dreamy" or "nostalgic" to evoke an ethereal feel.
- **Lighting**: "Soft daylight" helps create gentle contrasts typical of black and white photography.
- **Background**: Including "Art Deco architectural background" adds context and reinforces the 1920s setting.

You can adjust the prompt to add more specific elements or adjust the mood, such as incorporating more details about the lady's attire or expression, to better suit your creative vision.
