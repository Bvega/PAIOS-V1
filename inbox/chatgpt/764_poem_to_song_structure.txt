This poem beautifully conveys longing, love, and hope. It has a tender yet powerful emotional arc, perfect for a heartfelt ballad. Here’s a suggested **song structure** and melody idea for **“Un Día Cualquiera.”**

---

### **Song Structure**

#### **[Intro]** (Instrumental)  
A soft, melancholic instrumental opening, led by piano or acoustic guitar. The melody should feel reflective and wistful, like the first light of dawn.

---

#### **[Verse 1]**  
*Si un día cualquiera,  
Te acordaras que existo,  
Desearas mis besos,

- **Melody:** Slow and tender, with a descending note pattern to emphasize yearning.  
- **Rhythm:** Gentle 6/8 or 4/4 time signature, creating a swaying motion that mirrors the narrator's emotional vulnerability.  

---

#### **[Chorus]**  
*El tiempo transcurre, más te amo.  
No me resigno a perderte.  
Fui tuya, fuiste mío.

- **Melody:** A soaring melody, full of emotional peaks, contrasting with the softness of the verse. Let the words “¡cómo sufro!” stretch and linger for dramatic effect.  
- **Instruments:** Strings (violin or cello) swell to underscore the intensity, blending with the main instrument.  

---

#### **[Verse 2]**  
*Si un día cualquiera, me abrazaras,  
No podría negarme,

- **Melody:** Similar to Verse 1 but with slight variations to reflect hope growing amidst sorrow.  
- **Dynamics:** Add a soft percussion beat, like a heartbeat, symbolizing the narrator’s anticipation.  

---

#### **[Bridge]**

De este amor tan ardiente,

- **Melody:** Slow and haunting, with a minimalistic arrangement. Perhaps a solo instrument (e.g., violin or flute) plays softly in the background.  
- **Dynamic Change:** Strip back the music to focus on the vocal delivery, making it raw and intimate.  

---

#### **[Final Chorus]**
