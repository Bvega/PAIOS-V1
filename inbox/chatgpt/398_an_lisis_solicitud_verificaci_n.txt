ASSISTANT:
**MVP** stands for **Minimum Viable Product**.

It refers to the **simplest version of a product** that can be released with just enough features to be usable by early users, and to provide feedback for future development.

### 🔍 In other words:
- It’s the **first functional version** of your system.
- It focuses on the **core features only** (what’s essential for the product to work).
- It avoids building full functionality or polishing every detail upfront.

### ✅ Example in your case:
For the **Sansouci Ports Verification System**, the **MVP** could include only:
- BL lookup form
- Vehicle selection
- Date/time verification slot booking
- Submission confirmation
- Email confirmation

**No admin panel, reports, or advanced analytics**—those can come later based on real user feedback and needs.
