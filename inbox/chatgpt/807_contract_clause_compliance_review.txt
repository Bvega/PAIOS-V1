### 2. **Concessions and Special Considerations**

### 3. **Attrition Policy**
   - **Terms Observed**: Most contracts have an 80% minimum room block commitment, with fees applied for rooms below this threshold. Some, like Contract Model 002 Final, permit a 20% reduction without penalty【16†source】【24†source】.

### 4. **Payment and Billing Terms**
   - **Terms Observed**: Payment deadlines are specified (e.g., full payment seven days before the event in Contract Model 002). Most contracts mandate a credit card authorization for billing incidentals【18†source】【22†source】.
   - **Compliance Check**: The clause library suggests reinforcing these billing deadlines with consequences for late payments (interest rates or penalties).
   - **Recommendation**: To align with best practices, specify whether interest or fees will apply for late payments, ensuring it is explicitly stated in payment and billing clauses【23†source】.

### 5. **Cancellation Policy**
   - **Terms Observed**: Contracts generally outline scaled fees based on cancellation timing, with liquidated damages up to 100% within 29 days. This structure is visible across all reviewed documents.
   - **Compliance Check**: The terms are consistent with library examples by defining fees in proportion to the event’s proximity. However, specific language from the library regarding non-penal nature and intent to offset projected losses could enhance compliance【19†source】【24†source】.
   - **Recommendation**: For a stronger legal standing, add explicit language designating these fees as non-penal liquidated damages calculated based on prospective losses【18†source】【23†source】.

### 6. **Relocation Terms**
   - **Terms Observed**: Some contracts lack clear provisions about relocating guests if the hotel cannot fulfill reserved rooms. Where present, provisions vary in terms of alternative accommodations and the financial handling of guest relocation.
   - **Compliance Check**: For example, Contract Model 001 Final includes a comprehensive relocation clause, aligning well with industry standards by specifying nearby accommodation and transport for guests.
   - **Recommendation**: Where not detailed, include comprehensive relocation clauses to cover transport, comparable accommodations, and priority rebooking, as exemplified in the clause library【23†source】【25†source】.

### 7. **Alcohol and AV Policies**
   - **Terms Observed**: Contract Model 002 allows guests to bring their own alcohol with a corkage fee. Audio/Visual terms are exclusive to in-house providers with discounts (e.g., 10% for AV in Contract Model 003).
   - **Compliance Check**: These terms comply with library examples by stipulating in-house control over AV and alcohol services.
   - **Recommendation**: Ensure that alcohol service policies reinforce responsible service (ID checks for age verification) and AV provisions define acceptable external vendors if in-house services are restricted【20†source】【23†source】.

### Summary of Key Compliance Gaps
