## 🌿 **Seven Teas** – Concept Overview  
An **elegant, minimalist, and luxurious** tea brand website with a clean aesthetic, inspired by artisanal apothecary-style imagery. The user experience is focused, limited to **three pages**, built around storytelling, product elevation, and premium design.

---

### 🖋 **Branding**  
- **Name**: Seven Teas  
- **Logo**: Stylized **“7T”** monogram in serif type, engraved look  
- **Font style**: Elegant serif for titles (e.g., Playfair Display), minimalist sans-serif for body (e.g., Lato or Inter)  
- **Color palette**:
  - Deep Forest Green (#1B3B2F)
  - Antique White (#FAF3E0)
  - Warm Gold (#C6A664)
  - Charcoal Gray (#2F2F2F)

---

## 📄 PAGE STRUCTURE

### 1. **Home (Landing Page)**  
**Goal**: Showcase the elegance and story of Seven Teas

#### Layout
- **Hero Image**: Full-screen hero with background based on the concept image (rustic jars, dried herbs)
- **Overlay text**:  
  `Seven Teas` (centered, large serif font)  
  _“Crafted harmony in every leaf.”_  
  CTA button: `[ Explore Our Blends ]`

#### Sections
- **About the Ritual**  
  A short, elegant description of the philosophy of tea as a daily luxury.
  > “We believe tea is not just a drink. It’s a quiet ceremony, a luxurious pause.”

- **The 7 Signature Blends**  
  Display seven glass jar-style product cards with hover interactions showing:
  - Blend name
  - Key ingredients
  - Steeping notes
  - Subtle animations with gold accent underline

- **Call to Action**  
  Quote with a button:  
  > _“Discover your seventh sense.”_  
  `[ Shop the Collection ]`

---

### 2. **Our Blends**  
**Goal**: Provide detailed descriptions of each of the seven teas

#### Layout
- **Minimal grid or scroll-based design**  
- Each tea displayed in elegant cards with:
  - Name (e.g., “Golden Dawn”)
  - Image (styled like the jars in the concept photo)
  - Description: taste notes, origin, health benefits
  - Ingredients + Steeping instructions
  - Buy button

Optional: Filter or scroll navigation to jump between blends.

---

### 3. **Contact / Ritual Club**  
**Goal**: Subscription + exclusive club

#### Sections:
- **Join the Ritual Club**  
  - Minimal form (Name, Email)
  - Text: “Receive exclusive invites to tasting experiences, early product drops, and curated wellness rituals.”

- **Store Locator or Stockist Inquiry**  
  - Minimal form or static list if applicable

- **Social and Contact Info**  
  - Icons in soft gold or muted green
  - Footer with logo, privacy policy, terms

---

## ✨ Extra UX Notes
- **Scroll animations**: soft fade-ins and parallax jar movements
- **Audio**: Optional ambient tea pouring sounds on hover
- **Interactivity**: Light hover effects, microinteractions for luxury feel
