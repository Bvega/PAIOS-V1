For a headache, several types of teas may offer relief depending on the cause. Here are some recommendations:

1. **Peppermint Tea**: This is a great choice for tension headaches. The menthol in peppermint has a relaxing effect on muscles, which can help ease tension and reduce headache symptoms【7†source】.
   
2. **Ginger Tea**: Ginger is known for its anti-inflammatory properties, which can help alleviate pain associated with headaches, particularly migraines【7†source】.

3. **Green Tea**: Although green tea contains some caffeine, which may help reduce headache symptoms for those who benefit from a mild caffeine boost, it also contains antioxidants that may support overall well-being. However, be cautious if you are sensitive to caffeine, as it may trigger headaches in some individuals【8†source】【9†source】.

4. **Chamomile Tea**: Chamomile has a calming effect and can help relax muscles and alleviate stress, which may be beneficial for headaches related to tension or anxiety【9†source】.

Any of these options can help alleviate headache symptoms, and you might want to experiment with different teas to see which one works best for you. If the headache persists, consider consulting a healthcare professional.
