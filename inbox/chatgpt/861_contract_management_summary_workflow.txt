   - Check for comprehensive force majeure provisions.

4. **Detail Verification**
   - Confirm all contact information is accurate.
   - Ensure meeting room names and meal spaces are specified.
   - Look for any discrepancies or omissions.

   - Review deposit schedules and payment methods.
   - Make sure payment timelines are realistic for the client.

6. **Legal Clause Review**
   - Examine legal terms for compliance and client protection.
   - Use track changes to suggest amendments.
   - Consult with legal professionals if necessary.

7. **Client Consultation**
   - Present the reviewed contract to the client.
   - Explain key terms, potential risks, and suggested changes.
   - Clarify your role and recommend legal advice if needed.

   - Seek win-win solutions through open communication.

   - Incorporate all agreed-upon modifications.
   - Ensure all parties review the final version.

10. **Execute Contract**
