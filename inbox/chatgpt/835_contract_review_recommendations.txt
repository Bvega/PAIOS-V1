- **Current Clause**: The contract does not mention any resort fees or potential hidden charges.
- **Suggestion**: Ensure that all potential charges, such as resort fees, AV costs, or additional taxes, are explicitly outlined in the contract. Hidden charges should be avoided unless explicitly agreed upon in writing【16†source】【19†source】.

### 4. **Cut-Off Date:**
- **Current Clause**: The cut-off date is set to release unsold rooms for general sale without explicitly addressing group liability after that date.

### 5. **ADA Compliance:**
