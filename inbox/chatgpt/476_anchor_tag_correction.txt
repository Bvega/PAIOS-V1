Here's the corrected and completed anchor tag that links back to `index.html`:

```html
<p>home
    <a href="index.html" target="_self">Back to Home</a>
</p>
```

### Notes:
- The original tag had a malformed structure: the closing `>` for the `<a>` tag was missing.
- The `target="_index"` is not valid; use `"_self"` (default) if you want to open in the same tab.

If you just want the word **home** to be the clickable link:

```html
<p><a href="index.html">home</a></p>
```
