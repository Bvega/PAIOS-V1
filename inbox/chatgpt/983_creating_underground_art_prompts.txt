### Prompt Creation Steps

**Step 1: Identify the Core Visual Elements**
   - **Location and Setting:** Choose a setting that embodies the theme "UNDERGROUND" such as a dark cavern, a network of catacombs, or a hidden tunnel.
   - **Main Subjects:** Incorporate natural underground elements like snottites, mushrooms, stalactites, and stalagmites.
   - **Atmosphere and Mood:** Decide on the mood, whether it's eerie, mysterious, or serene, using descriptors like "shadowy," "dimly lit," or "glowing with bioluminescence."

**Step 2: Integrate the Keywords**
   - Use each keyword to add specific details. For example:
     - **Snottites:** Describe them as "slimy, acidic formations clinging to the ceiling."
     - **Cavern:** "A vast, echoing cavern filled with stalactites and stalagmites."
     - **Mushrooms:** "Giant bioluminescent mushrooms casting a soft glow."
     - **Stalactites and Stalagmites:** Use both to create a balanced underground landscape.
     - **Catacombs:** "Endless catacombs with hidden secrets and ancient relics."
     - **Aquifer:** "Crystal-clear underground pools fed by a natural aquifer."
     - **Subterranean:** Emphasize depth and isolation with "subterranean abyss."
     - **Tunnel:** Describe pathways like "narrow, winding tunnels."
     - **Abyss:** Add a sense of scale with "a yawning abyss that seems endless."
     - **Secret:** Include hidden or concealed elements to add intrigue.

**Step 3: Add Artistic and Style Elements**
   - **Art Style:** Choose a style like photorealistic, digital painting, or fantasy art.
   - **Color Scheme:** Decide on a color palette, such as dark blues, greens, and subtle glows to emphasize the underground theme.
   - **Perspective and Composition:** Choose perspectives that enhance the setting, like a wide-angle view of a cavern or a close-up of snottites.

**Step 4: Refine the Prompt**
   - Combine all elements into a cohesive and vivid description. Be specific about the scene and use the words creatively to ensure they naturally fit into the narrative.

**Example Prompts:**

1. **"A photorealistic depiction of an underground cavern with towering stalactites and stalagmites, glowing bioluminescent mushrooms scattered around, and slimy snottites hanging from the ceiling. A narrow tunnel leads into an abyss, partially illuminated by a hidden aquifer reflecting light onto the walls. Secrets of ancient catacombs lie in the shadows, adding an air of mystery and danger."**

2. **"A digital painting of a subterranean world featuring a vast, echoing cavern filled with crystals and stalagmites. Giant mushrooms with bioluminescent caps provide a soft blue glow, contrasting with the dark, shadowy recesses of the catacombs in the background. Stalactites drip slowly into an underground aquifer, creating ripples in the otherwise still water, while a hidden tunnel suggests a secret passageway deeper into the earth."**

3. **"In the heart of a subterranean abyss, a secret catacomb is revealed, with ancient relics peeking through the shadows. The scene is illuminated by bioluminescent mushrooms and crystal formations that catch the light. Stalactites and stalagmites create a natural barrier, while a tunnel winds its way through the cavern, leading to a hidden aquifer that sparkles in the dim light."**
