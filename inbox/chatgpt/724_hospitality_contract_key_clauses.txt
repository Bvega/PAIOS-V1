The main clauses to consider in a hospitality contract typically include the following:

1. **Guest Room Accommodations**:
   - Details about room blocks, rates, and any additional charges such as taxes or resort fees【14†source】【15†source】.
   - Policies for early check-in, late check-out, or additional room bookings【15†source】【19†source】.

2. **Attrition Clauses**:
   - Specifies the group’s commitment to booking a minimum percentage of the contracted rooms or facilities. Failure to meet the minimum can result in penalties【15†source】【16†source】【20†source】.

3. **Concessions**:
   - Includes complimentary amenities, discounts on services, or waivers on specific fees as part of the contract【14†source】【17†source】【22†source】.

4. **Reservation Procedures and Cutoff Dates**:
   - Outlines the methods of reservation and the deadline for group bookings at the contracted rates【15†source】【16†source】【21†source】.

5. **Billing and Payment Terms**:
   - Details regarding deposits, payment schedules, and accepted payment methods【16†source】【22†source】.

6. **Cancellation and Termination Policies**:
   - Defines the penalties for cancellation based on the notice period and the anticipated revenue【16†source】【20†source】【22†source】.

7. **Force Majeure**:
   - Protects both parties from liabilities due to unforeseen events that prevent the fulfillment of contractual obligations【14†source】.

8. **Damage and Liability Clauses**:
   - Covers responsibility for any damages caused to the venue by the group and vice versa【14†source】【16†source】.

9. **Compliance Requirements**:
   - ADA compliance or other legal stipulations such as alcohol service policies【14†source】【15†source】.

10. **Concessions and Additional Fees**:
   - Ensures transparency for additional fees (e.g., AV equipment, corkage) and specifies pre-agreed charges【14†source】【17†source】.

11. **Relocation Policy**:
   - Establishes the hotel's responsibility in case guests cannot be accommodated due to overbooking【15†source】【21†source】.

12. **Insurance and Indemnity**:
   - Covers liability, including insurance requirements for both the group and the hotel【14†source】【15†source】.
