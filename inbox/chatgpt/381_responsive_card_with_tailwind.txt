### Key Additions:
- `text-[size]` changes at `sm`, `md`, and `lg` breakpoints.
- `p-[value]` and `my-[value]` scale up on larger screens.
- `max-w-[value]` increases width adaptively.
- `mt-4 md:mt-0` allows vertical stacking on mobile, horizontal on desktop.
