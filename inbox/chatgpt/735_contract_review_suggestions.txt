### Recommendations for Improvements:

#### 1. **Force Majeure Clause**:
Include more comprehensive language for events like pandemics, government regulations, or natural disasters:
   - *"In the event of a force majeure, neither party shall be liable for failure to perform its obligations under this agreement. Force majeure shall include, but is not limited to, acts of God, government orders, pandemics, labor strikes, and other unforeseen circumstances beyond the control of the parties."*

#### 2. **Data Privacy and GRIP Compliance**:
   - *"Both parties shall comply with all applicable data protection laws. The Group agrees to obtain necessary attendee permissions for sharing data with the Hotel. The Hotel agrees to safeguard any shared information and will not use it for purposes outside the scope of this Agreement without prior written consent."*

#### 3. **Flexibility in Attrition and Cancellation**:
   - Adjust attrition clauses to offer a tiered penalty system based on the percentage of unfulfilled room blocks.
   - For cancellations:
     *"If cancellation occurs due to circumstances beyond the Group’s control (e.g., travel bans, natural disasters), the Group shall not be liable for cancellation fees beyond documented recoverable expenses incurred by the Hotel."*

#### 4. **Payment Terms**:
   - Add explicit schedules for deposits, intermediate payments, and final payments to prevent disputes.
   - *"The Group shall pay 25% of the total estimated cost within 30 days of contract execution, 50% six months prior to the event, and the remaining balance 30 days prior to the event date."*

#### 5. **Audit Transparency**:
   - Include a clear indemnity for data-related breaches during GRIP execution.
   - *"Hotel and Group agree to limit liability in the event of unintentional data breaches resulting from GRIP use, provided both parties employ reasonable security measures."*

#### 6. **Additional Concessions**:
   - Offer flexibility to increase/decrease room blocks within a specific timeframe.
   - *"The Group may increase or reduce the room block by 10% without penalty up to 90 days prior to the event, subject to availability."*

#### 7. **Improved Indemnification**:
   - Ensure it applies to both parties equitably.
   - *"Both parties agree to indemnify and hold harmless the other from claims arising from their own negligence or breach of contract."*

#### 8. **Resort Fees**:
   - Add clarity on how resort fees apply to attendees and their optional nature.
   - *"Resort fees will be disclosed in attendee booking confirmations and are optional unless specified by the Group."*

#### 9. **Review Clause Library**:
Compare against the Clause Library document to ensure all modern trends and inclusions are captured (e.g., ADA compliance, environmental sustainability measures, arbitration for disputes).
