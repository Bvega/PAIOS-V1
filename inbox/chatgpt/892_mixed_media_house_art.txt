ASSISTANT:
To create a prompt for a **mixed media depiction** combining **watercolor** and **color marker techniques**, of a **modern two-story minimalist house** nestled within a **dense forest at dusk**, you could structure it as follows:

---

**Prompt:** 

"Create a mixed media artwork combining watercolor and color marker techniques, depicting a modern, two-story minimalist house with sleek metal and wood construction. The house features floor-to-ceiling glass windows, reflecting the towering trees surrounding it, blending seamlessly into the dense forest at dusk. Warm indoor lighting from the house contrasts against the cool, darkening forest, casting a peaceful and tranquil mood. Outdoor lounge chairs sit on a natural stone patio, framed by lush greenery. The watercolor technique should capture the fluidity and softness of the natural environment, while color markers define the clean, sharp lines of the house, emphasizing its modern design. The lighting should gently glow through the reflective windows, enhancing the serene, calm atmosphere."

---

This prompt takes into account both techniques and the contrast between the sleek, sharp lines of modern architecture and the natural, organic environment surrounding it. The mixed media approach helps highlight these different elements effectively.
