Here’s a layout design plan for a radio station website that publishes AI-generated songs from human-written poems:

---

## **Website Layout Design**

### **1. Header**
- **Logo**: Positioned on the left with a modern and sleek design, showcasing the radio station's name and a symbol/icon representing AI and creativity (e.g., a quill with digital notes).
- **Navigation Menu**: Centered or aligned to the right, with the following tabs:
  - **Home**
  - **Discover Poems**
  - **Submit Your Poem**
  - **About Us**
  - **Contact**
- **Search Bar**: Subtle and aligned to the top right, with a magnifying glass icon.

---

### **2. Hero Section**
- **Background**: Vibrant gradient or dynamic animation representing sound waves or musical notes.
- **Headline**: Large and bold text, e.g., *“Turning Poems into Melodies: A Fusion of Art and AI.”*
- **Subheadline**: Smaller text, e.g., *“Discover AI-generated songs inspired by human creativity.”*
- **Call-to-Action Buttons**:
  - **Listen Now**
  - **Submit Your Poem**

---

### **3. Featured Poems & Songs Section**
- **Title**: *“Featured Creations”*
- **Content Layout**:
  - A **carousel** or **grid** showcasing featured poems and their corresponding songs.
  - Each item includes:
    - Poem Title
    - Poem Author (linked to their profile)
    - A short excerpt of the poem
    - Play Button for the song
  - Interactive visual elements, such as dynamic audio waveforms or glowing effects when hovered over.
- **Highlight Animation**: As songs play, lyrics sync in a karaoke-style animation beneath the play button.

---

### **4. Poem & Song Viewer**
- When a poem/song is selected:
  - **Full View Layout**:
    - **Left Panel**:
      - Display the **poem text**, styled with a modern, elegant typography.
      - Karaoke-style lyric highlights synced with the song.
    - **Right Panel**:
      - Audio Player with controls (play, pause, rewind, etc.).
      - **Song Progress Visual**: Animated lyrics in real-time, mirroring karaoke style.
    - **Author Details**:
      - Photo of the author (optional)
      - Name, short bio, and history of their works
      - Links to their other published poems or external profiles (if provided).

---

### **5. Submit Your Poem Section**
- **Title**: *“Let Your Words Sing”*
- **Instructions**:
  - Clear steps to submit a poem.
  - Form fields for:
    - Poem Title
    - Poem Text
    - Author Name
    - Optional Bio and Photo Upload
- **Design**: Clean and minimalistic with a focus on engagement (e.g., motivational phrases like *“Your creativity inspires us!”*).

---

### **6. Discover Poems Section**
- **Filter Options**:
  - By Genre: Romantic, Inspirational, Melancholic, etc.
  - By Author
  - By Date Added
- **Interactive Display**:
  - Clickable cards for each poem/song combo with preview snippets.
  - Tags for genres/themes.

---

### **7. Footer**
- **Content**:
  - Quick Links: Privacy Policy, Terms of Service, FAQs
  - Social Media Icons (vibrant hover effects): Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, YouTube
  - Contact Information
  - Newsletter Signup: *“Stay Tuned for New Creations!”*
- **Background**: Contrasting color with subtle patterns or gradients to keep the footer engaging.

---

### **8. Additional Features**
- **Dark/Light Mode Toggle**: To cater to user preferences and accessibility.
- **Responsive Design**: Ensuring seamless functionality on desktops, tablets, and mobile devices.
- **Animation Effects**: Smooth transitions, hover animations, and micro-interactions for an engaging user experience.
- **Modern Fonts**: Use bold, readable fonts like Montserrat for titles and Lora or Roboto for body text.

---

### **Color Palette**
- **Primary**: Vibrant tones like teal, coral, or magenta.
- **Secondary**: Subtle pastel gradients or monochromatic shades for backgrounds.
- **Accent**: Bright highlights for CTAs and interactive elements.
