You can generate music videos from your song (lyrics + mp3 or wav) using a combination of AI tools for **lyric-based visuals**, **audio sync**, and **video editing**. Here’s a structured breakdown:

---

## 1. **Automatic AI Video Generators**
These platforms let you upload your song (mp3/wav) and add lyrics to generate visuals:
- **Runway Gen-2** – Upload audio and prompts to generate cinematic visuals that sync with your music.
- **Kaiber.ai** – Specializes in making music videos; you can upload your track and lyrics, choose a style, and it generates a full video.
- **Pika Labs** – Text-to-video AI with music sync features; works well with short clips.
- **Rotor Videos** – Upload your track, add lyrics, and it will automatically cut visuals to the beat.

---

## 2. **Lyric Video Creation**
If you want the lyrics animated on-screen (karaoke or dynamic style):
- **After Effects (with plugins like Lyric Video Template)** – Full control, but more advanced.
- **CapCut / Canva Pro** – Easier drag-and-drop option for syncing lyrics with your track.
- **Veed.io** – Upload audio, auto-generate subtitles, and style them as animated lyrics.

---

## 3. **Custom Workflow**
If you want maximum flexibility:
1. **Prepare Assets**  
   - Your audio file (mp3/wav).  
   - The lyrics (text file).  
   - Optional: reference images, mood board, or video clips.  

2. **Generate Visuals**  
   - Use **Stable Video Diffusion** or **Runway Gen-2** with text prompts (e.g., “dreamy neon cityscape, animated to match a sad ballad”).  

3. **Sync with Audio**  
   - Import everything into **CapCut**, **DaVinci Resolve**, or **Adobe Premiere Pro**.  
   - Place generated visuals on the timeline, sync with song beats.  
   - Add lyric overlays using subtitle import or manual keyframes.  

4. **Export**  
   - Export in MP4 format (1080p or 4K depending on your need).  

---

## 4. **Beginner-Friendly Path**
If you’re starting out and want the fastest route:
1. Upload your song to **Kaiber.ai** or **Rotor Videos**.  
2. Paste your lyrics in their lyric-sync tool.  
3. Pick a visual style (anime, cinematic, abstract, etc.).  
4. Render → Download → Share.
