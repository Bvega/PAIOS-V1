### **Impasto-Style Painting Template**

**Prompt Structure:**
"Create a highly textured, impasto-style painting of [subject] in a [environment/setting]. The brushstrokes should be thick and pronounced, giving the painting a three-dimensional, sculptural effect. Use vibrant colors like [color 1], [color 2], and [color 3] to highlight [specific features or elements], capturing a [mood/emotion] moment."

**Example Prompt:**
"Create a highly textured, impasto-style painting of a smiling child playing in a muddy field. The brushstrokes should be thick and pronounced, giving the painting a three-dimensional, sculptural effect. Use vibrant colors like blues, browns, and whites to highlight the child's wet hair and muddy surroundings, capturing a lively and carefree moment."

---

### **Template Elements:**

- **[subject]:** Describe the main focus of the painting, such as a person, animal, or object (e.g., "a child," "a dog," "a bouquet of flowers").
- **[environment/setting]:** Specify the location or context in which the subject is placed (e.g., "a muddy field," "a vibrant garden," "a bustling city street").
- **[color 1], [color 2], [color 3]:** Choose the primary colors to emphasize in the painting (e.g., "reds, yellows, and greens" for a garden, or "grays, blues, and blacks" for a cityscape).
- **[specific features or elements]:** Highlight any particular aspects of the subject or environment (e.g., "the child's wet hair," "the dog's wagging tail," "the flowers' petals").
- **[mood/emotion]:** Describe the feeling or atmosphere you want the painting to convey (e.g., "lively and carefree," "calm and serene," "energetic and bustling").

---

This template can be adapted for a wide variety of subjects and settings while maintaining the distinctive impasto painting style.
