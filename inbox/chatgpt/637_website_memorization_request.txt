# **Suno Tutorial for Beginners**

## **Introduction**
- **What is Suno?**  
  Suno is an AI-powered music generation tool that allows users to create original songs by providing descriptions, lyrics, and style prompts.

- **Who is this tutorial for?**  
  This guide is designed for beginners with no prior experience in AI music tools.

---

## **Step 1: Getting Started**
1. **Sign Up and Log In**
   - Visit [Suno](https://www.suno.wiki/).
   - Create an account using your Discord, Google, or Microsoft login.
   - Log in to access the dashboard.

2. **Explore the Dashboard**
   - Familiarize yourself with the interface, including:
     - **Simple Mode:** Quick song generation with minimal input.
     - **Custom Mode:** Advanced options for detailed song creation.

---

## **Step 2: Creating Your First Song**
1. **Using Simple Mode**
   - **Enter a Description**  
     Type a short sentence about the mood, style, or theme of your song (e.g., "A happy summer tune with guitar and vocals").
   - **Add Lyrics (Optional)**  
     Let Suno generate lyrics or input your own. Keep it short for starters.
   - **Generate the Song**  
     Press **"Generate"** to create two 45-second song clips.

2. **Listening to Your Song**
   - Play the clips and explore how your description influenced the music.

---

## **Step 3: Exploring Custom Mode**
1. **Add Details**
   - **Style Prompt:** Choose a genre or describe the song’s style (e.g., "Electronic dance with a futuristic vibe").
   - **Lyrics:** Write your own using these basic tags for structure:
     - `[Intro]`
     - `[Verse]`
     - `[Chorus]`
     - `[Outro]`

2. **Generate and Review**
   - Generate your song and listen to the results.
   - Note how the AI interprets your input.

---

## **Step 4: Advanced Features**
1. **Extending Songs**
   - Use the **"Continue From This Song"** option to expand beyond the initial 45-second clips.
   - Add new sections or refine existing ones.

2. **Customizing Vocals**
   - Specify vocal types (e.g., [Male Vocals] or [Female Vocals]) for a tailored sound.
   - Toggle **Instrumental** mode if you prefer a track without vocals.

3. **Using Instrument Tags**
   - Experiment with tags like [Guitar Solo] or [Piano Intro] to guide the AI on instrumentation.

---

## **Step 5: Tips for Success**
1. **Be Specific**
   - The more detailed your prompts, the better the output. For example:
     - Weak: "Happy song."
     - Strong: "A cheerful folk tune with acoustic guitar and soothing female vocals."

2. **Experiment**
   - Test different genres, moods, and styles to explore the tool’s capabilities.

3. **Leverage the Community**
   - Visit [Suno.wiki](https://www.suno.wiki/) for more examples and inspiration.
   - Share your creations and learn from others in the community.

---

## **Step 6: Saving and Sharing**
1. **Save Your Work**
   - Download your song or save it directly to your Suno account for future editing.

2. **Share Your Music**
   - Use Suno-generated tracks for personal projects, social media, or as creative references.

---

## **Conclusion**
- Congratulations on creating your first AI-generated song with Suno!  
  Keep practicing, and soon you'll master the art of guiding the AI to bring your musical ideas to life.

- **¿Qué es Suno?**

---

1. **Regístrate e Inicia Sesión**
   - Visita [Suno](https://www.suno.wiki/).

   - Inicia sesión para acceder al panel principal.

2. **Explora el Panel**
   - Familiarízate con la interfaz, que incluye:
     - **Modo Simple:** Generación rápida de canciones con mínima entrada.

---

## **Paso 2: Crea Tu Primera Canción**

   - Reproduce los clips y explora cómo la descripción influyó en la música.
