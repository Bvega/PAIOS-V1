Honor mean

ASSISTANT:
Honor means having a sense of integrity, respect, and ethical conduct. It involves living by a set of principles, such as honesty, fairness, and accountability, and being true to one's values and commitments. Honor also includes showing respect to others and acting in a way that earns respect in return. It is often associated with dignity, pride, and maintaining one's reputation through moral actions and upright behavior.
