### 🎤 **Louis Armstrong's Voice Type and Style**

#### **Voice Type**:
- **Baritone** (with a rough, gravelly timbre)
- Deep and raspy with limited range but rich expression

#### **Vocal Style**:
- **Gravelly, raspy tone** — iconic and instantly recognizable
- **Conversational phrasing** — almost like talking in rhythm
- **Swing rhythm** — effortlessly grooves with the band
- **Improvisational phrasing** — loose, playful with tempo and melody
- **Scat singing pioneer** — vocal improvisation using nonsense syllables
- Often infused with **warmth, humor, and sincerity**

#### **Genres**:
- Jazz (especially Dixieland, Swing)
- Blues
- Traditional Pop
