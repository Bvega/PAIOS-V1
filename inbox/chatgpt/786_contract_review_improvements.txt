Based on a thorough review of the contract (Contract Model 002 Final) and its comparisons to both previous versions and the clause library, here are several suggested improvements with explanations:

1. **Attrition Clause**
   - **Current Clause**: The group can reduce contracted rooms by 20% without penalty. Below this threshold, the group must meet 80% of total room nights or pay for the difference.
   - **Suggested Clause**: Include a clarification on re-sell credits for unused rooms. For example, "Any rooms re-sold by the hotel after receiving notice of room block reduction will be credited against the Group’s unused rooms."
   - **Reason**: This aligns with best practices and allows the group to potentially mitigate attrition fees if the hotel is able to resell unused rooms.

2. **Cancellation Policy**
   - **Current Clause**: Specific cancellation fees apply based on notice dates.
   - **Suggested Clause**: Strengthen this clause by defining "cancellation charges" as liquidated damages, not penalties. An example clause from the library includes: "These cancellation charges are not a penalty but a reasonable estimation of Hotel’s loss."
   - **Reason**: This language, which specifies that fees are for liquidated damages, provides clarity and legal protection against disputes about penalty vs. damages.

3. **Force Majeure Clause**
