USER:
If I earn 100,000 dollars per year what’s my hour of work worth ?

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

ASSISTANT:
To calculate your hourly wage based on an annual salary of $100,000, you'll need to divide your salary by the number of work hours in a year. Assuming you work a standard full-time schedule of 40 hours per week for 52 weeks per year, the calculation is as follows:

\[
\frac{100,000}{40 \times 52} = \frac{100,000}{2,080} \approx 48.08
\]

Your hourly wage would be approximately $48.08.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

USER:
And by the minute? Based on this calculation

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

ASSISTANT:
To calculate your earnings per minute based on the hourly wage of $48.08, divide this by 60 (the number of minutes in an hour):

\[
\frac{48.08}{60} \approx 0.80
\]

So, your earnings per minute would be approximately $0.80.
