where can i gather hospitality contract databases

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

ASSISTANT:
You can gather hospitality contract databases from the following sources:

1. **Government Portals**: Some countries or states have government portals that publish hospitality contracts, including contracts for hotel management, tourism services, and catering services. Examples include:
   - The **United States General Services Administration (GSA)** portal.
   - **EU Tenders Electronic Daily (TED)** for hospitality-related contracts in the European Union.

2. **Hospitality Industry Associations**: Associations like the **American Hotel & Lodging Association (AHLA)** or **International Hotel & Restaurant Association (IH&RA)** may have resources or access to contract databases.

3. **Legal Databases**: Platforms like **LexisNexis**, **Westlaw**, or **Practical Law** often include contract templates and samples for various industries, including hospitality. Subscription is usually required for full access.

4. **Business-to-Business (B2B) Platforms**: Platforms like **Contracts Counsel**, **LawInsider**, or **Rocket Lawyer** offer contract templates and databases for different sectors, including hospitality management agreements, event service contracts, and more.

5. **Freelancer Legal Services**: Websites like **UpCounsel** or **LegalZoom** may provide access to a variety of hospitality contract templates and legal advice for customization.

6. **University Libraries and Research Institutions**: Some academic institutions with hospitality or tourism programs maintain databases with industry contracts or research papers related to hospitality law.

7. **Consulting Firms**: Hospitality consulting companies like **HVS**, **CBRE Hotels**, and **Horwath HTL** may have proprietary databases or offer reports that include contract information relevant to hospitality ventures.

8. **Public Domain Contracts**: You can also find examples of public domain hospitality contracts in archives, industry reports, or government procurement documents.

By leveraging these sources, you can compile a comprehensive collection of hospitality contracts for research or practical use.
