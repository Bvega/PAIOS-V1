ASSISTANT:
To batch delete timestamps like `[00:10.320 --> 00:14.160]` in Word, follow these steps:

1. **Open Microsoft Word** and the document where you want to remove the timestamps.

2. **Go to the "Home" tab**, and then click on **"Replace"** (or press `Ctrl + H`).

3. In the **"Find what"** field, enter this pattern to match the timestamps:
   ```
   \[[0-9]{2}:[0-9]{2}\.[0-9]{3} --> [0-9]{2}:[0-9]{2}\.[0-9]{3}\]
   ```

4. Leave the **"Replace with"** field empty if you want to erase the timestamps.

5. Click on **"More"**, and check the box for **"Use wildcards"**.

6. Finally, click **"Replace All"** to remove all occurrences of the timestamp format from the document.

This method will batch erase all timestamps formatted like `[00:10.320 --> 00:14.160]` in your Word document.
