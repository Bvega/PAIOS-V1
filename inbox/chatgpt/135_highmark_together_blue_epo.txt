Highmark Together Blue EPO is a type of health insurance plan offered by Highmark that provides benefits within a contracted network of providers. As an Exclusive Provider Organization (EPO), it generally does not cover out-of-network care, except for emergency or urgent services, and does not require referrals for specialists. These plans are available on the Affordable Care Act marketplace and come in different metal tiers, often including adult dental and vision benefits, and offer access to nationwide care through the BlueCard Program for emergencies and urgent care. 

Key Characteristics of Together Blue EPO
	•	Network-based: You must use providers within Highmark's contracted network for services to be covered.      
	•	No Referrals Needed: You do not need a referral from a primary care physician to see a specialist within the network.      
	•	Out-of-Network Coverage: Only emergency and urgent care services are covered when you are outside of the network.      
	•	Tiered Plans: The plans are available in different metal tiers (e.g., Bronze, Silver, Gold) which affect the costs you share with Highmark for your healthcare.      
	•	Digital Tools: You can access your plan information, find doctors, and track your deductible through the My Highmark app or website.      
	•	Telemedicine: Access to virtual care services, like virtual visits with nurses or health coaches, is often included.      
	•	Included Benefits: Many plans include adult dental and vision benefits, eliminating the need for separate insurance policies for these services.      
	•	BlueCard Program: For urgent and emergency care when traveling or living outside the U.S., the BlueCard® Program provides access to a nationwide network of providers.      
How to Find a Plan
	•	Explore Highmark's Website: Visit shop.highmark.com to view current plans and benefits.

---

### 🔑 **Key Features**

  Services are covered only if you use doctors and hospitals within Highmark’s contracted network.

- **👨‍⚕️ No Specialist Referrals Required**

  Through the **BlueCard® Program**, you can access urgent/emergency care anywhere in the U.S.

## **Highmark Together Blue EPO – Resumen General**

**Highmark Together Blue EPO** es un plan de salud del tipo **Organización de Proveedores Exclusivos (EPO)** ofrecido a través del **mercado de seguros médicos de la Ley de Cuidado de Salud Asequible (ACA)**. Está diseñado para ofrecer atención médica **solo dentro de la red** (excepto en emergencias) e incluye beneficios adicionales como **cobertura dental, de visión y herramientas digitales de salud**.

---

### 🔑 **Características Principales**

- **🔷 Atención Solo Dentro de la Red**  
  Los servicios están cubiertos únicamente si usas médicos y hospitales que pertenezcan a la red contratada por Highmark.  
  _Excepción: atención de emergencia o urgente está cubierta fuera de la red._

- **👨‍⚕️ No Se Requieren Referencias para Especialistas**  
  Puedes agendar directamente con cualquier especialista dentro de la red, sin necesidad de autorización previa de un médico de cabecera.

- **🏥 Cobertura Nacional para Emergencias/Urgencias**  
  A través del **programa BlueCard®**, puedes acceder a atención urgente o de emergencia en cualquier parte de EE. UU.

- **📊 Planes por Niveles**  
  Los planes están organizados por **niveles metálicos** (Bronce, Plata, Oro), que afectan las primas, deducibles y costos compartidos.

- **🦷👓 Beneficios Adicionales Incluidos**  
  Muchos planes incluyen cobertura para **dientes y visión de adultos**, evitando la necesidad de pólizas adicionales.

- **📱 Acceso Digital**  
  Usa el sitio web o app **My Highmark** para:
  - Encontrar doctores dentro de la red
  - Seguir tus reclamos y deducible
  - Acceder a tu tarjeta de seguro digital

- **💻 Telemedicina y Asistencia Virtual**  
  Acceso a visitas virtuales con enfermeros, entrenadores de salud y otros servicios remotos de atención médica.
