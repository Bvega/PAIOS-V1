ASSISTANT:
Here are the **top AI platforms** currently leading for creating **music videos**, depending on your goals (e.g., lip sync, animation, visualizer, storytelling, or editing):

---

### **1. Pika Labs**
- **Best for**: AI-generated animated music videos, cinematic sequences.
- **Strengths**: Text-to-video + image-to-video; high-quality motion and style control.
- **Use Case**: Turn lyrics or mood into stylized video scenes.

---

### **2. Runway ML (Gen-2)**
- **Best for**: Artistic, realistic AI-generated video clips.
- **Strengths**: Text, image, and video inputs; style transfer and editing.
- **Use Case**: Generate visuals based on mood, lyrics, or narrative.

---

### **3. Kaiber**
- **Best for**: Music visualizers and stylized animation synced to audio.
- **Strengths**: Audio-reactive visuals; ideal for musicians.
- **Use Case**: Upload your track, get generative art/music video out.

---

### **4. Animoto**
- **Best for**: Quick, professional-style lyric/music videos using templates.
- **Strengths**: Drag-and-drop; business-oriented.
- **Use Case**: Fast music videos or promo content with minimal effort.

---

### **5. DeepMotion or Move.ai**
- **Best for**: AI-driven motion capture for performance videos.
- **Strengths**: Turn real performance into 3D avatar motion.
- **Use Case**: Create dance or performance-based animated videos.

---

### **6. Synthesia**
- **Best for**: Lip-sync avatars or AI singers in music videos.
- **Strengths**: Talking/singing avatars from script + voice.
- **Use Case**: Virtual singers for low-budget music videos.

---

### **7. Deforum (Stable Diffusion animation)**
- **Best for**: Custom, psychedelic and surreal music video art.
- **Strengths**: Full control via prompt + settings; open-source.
- **Use Case**: Experimental, fully custom animated music visuals.

---

### **Best Combo Workflow Example**
For full control and pro results:
1. **Lyrics to visuals**: Runway or Pika Labs
2. **Visuals editing/compositing**: Adobe Premiere or DaVinci Resolve
3. **Lip-sync/avatar**: Synthesia or DeepMotion
4. **Finalize effects**: After Effects + audio-sync
