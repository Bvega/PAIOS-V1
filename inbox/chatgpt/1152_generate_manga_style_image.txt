### Step-by-Step Guide to Generate a Manga Style Image

1. **Determine the Demographic Style**:
   - **Shōnen**: Targeted at young boys (12-18). Features action, adventure, humor, and strong male protagonists.
   - **Shōjo**: Targeted at young girls (10-18). Focuses on romance, friendship, and coming-of-age stories.
   - **Seinen**: Aimed at adult men (18-30). Deals with mature themes and complex plots.
   - **Josei**: Aimed at adult women (18-30). Centers on realistic and relatable themes.
   - **Kodomo**: For young children. Simple stories and easy-to-understand artwork.

2. **Select an Artistic Style**:
   - **Big Eyes and Expressive Faces**: Common in Shōjo and Kodomo for a cute and endearing appearance.
   - **Dynamic Action Sequences**: Popular in Shōnen for depicting battles and fights.
   - **Detailed Backgrounds**: Creates atmosphere and immersion, often seen in Seinen and Josei.
   - **Minimalist Backgrounds**: Focuses on characters and dialogue, can be used across various demographics.
   - **Realistic Art**: Can be found in Seinen for a more serious tone.

3. **Choose a Theme or Genre**:
   - **Action**: Physical activities and heroics.
   - **Romance**: Relationships and emotional conflicts.
   - **Horror**: Designed to frighten and evoke dread.
   - **Science Fiction**: Futuristic or imagined innovations.
   - **Fantasy**: Magical or supernatural elements.

4. **Describe the Scene**:
   - **Characters**: Include details about their appearance, clothing, and expressions.
   - **Setting**: Describe the environment, whether it’s a bustling city, a magical forest, or a futuristic landscape.
   - **Mood**: Specify the overall mood, such as cheerful, tense, romantic, or eerie.

### Example Prompt for Image Generation

Let’s say you want to create a Shōnen manga-style image with dynamic action sequences. Here’s how you might describe it:

- **Demographic**: Shōnen

- **Setting**: A futuristic cityscape with tall skyscrapers and neon lights.
- **Mood**: Intense and energetic.
